Info About The Order

Would you like to purchase 3D animation from us?

1
Application

Send us an email with a request for animation, specify the duration, the price you are willing to pay, which models will be used, and we will then respond to you.

2
Consultation

If we accept your application, we will contact you through third-party networks (Telegram or Discord) and discuss all the details.

3
Getting Started

We confirm the agreements, you make an advance payment (it is negotiable) and we begin work.

4
Process

Animation, rendering, post-production – you get intermediate results

5
Final render

Delivery of the finished project in the required format and quality
