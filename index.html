Commissioning Process

Ready to manifest your project into digital reality? Here is our roadmap from the first draft to the final sequence.

1
The Spark (Application)

Drop us a detailed brief outlining your target duration, technical parameters (models/rigs required), and proposed budget. We'll analyze the feasibility and follow up swiftly.

2
Deep Dive (Consultation)

Once accepted, we establish a secure project hub on Discord or Telegram to break down creative direction, storyboarding, and reference keys.

3
Greenlight (Advance)

With contracts and parameters locked down, work officially initiates upon receipt of an adjustable deposit designed to secure your production slot.

4
The Pipeline (Process)

Watch it grow live. Receive recurring sneak-peeks, blockouts, pre-vis sheets, and intermediate edits to ensure the execution aligns with your exact vision.

5
Final Master (Delivery)

We deploy final lighting, post-processing, and multi-format delivery, providing high-bitrate master files tailored exactly to your destination specifications.
