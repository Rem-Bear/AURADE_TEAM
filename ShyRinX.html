About Me

Heyo! My name is ShyRinX and I'm the Head of AURADE Studio. I specialize in 3D animation, modeling, and design. With years of experience in the industry, I lead our talented team to create stunning visual content.

I've been producing 3D animations and visual effects for over 5 years, working on various projects ranging from short films to music videos. My work has been featured across multiple platforms and has gathered millions of views.

Skill Tree

As the Head of Studio, my primary focus is on delivering high-quality 3D animation and design. My toolset includes:

Blender 3D Source Filmmaker 3D Modeling Texturing Rigging Animation Lighting Rendering Compositing Adobe Photoshop Adobe After Effects

I'm constantly learning and improving my skills. The world of 3D is vast and there's always something new to discover!

How I got Here

My journey into 3D animation started when I discovered Source Filmmaker. I was fascinated by the ability to create cinematic scenes and tell stories through animation. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a passion.

Over the years, I've worked with various communities and creators, honing my craft and building a network of talented individuals. This eventually led to the founding of AURADE Studio.
